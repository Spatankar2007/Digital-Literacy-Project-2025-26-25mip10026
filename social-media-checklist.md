**_*Social Media Safety Checklist*_**

* **The brief requires a list of at least 5 points to ensure a safe and professional social media presence.**

* **Privacy Audit: Check your account settings to see who can view your posts.**

* **Tagging Review: Enable "Review Tags" so others cannot post photos to your profile without permission.**

* **App Permissions: Regularly remove third-party apps that have access to your social media data.**

* **Think Before Posting: Ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future employer seeing the post.**

* **Secure Login: Ensure your mobile number and recovery email are up to date for account recovery.**
